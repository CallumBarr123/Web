About us


About Us

The Record Room was founded by Elliot and Mara—lifelong friends brought together by a love of vinyl, late-night music conversations, and the simple joy of bringing a turntable back to life. Elliot’s passion for collecting records and craving for a trustworthy, personal place to buy used vinyl paired naturally with Mara’s knack for repairing audio equipment and restoring turntables with care. On a rainy afternoon over coffee, the idea for a small online shop built on quality, transparency, and genuine love for analog sound came to life. Today, every record is hand-selected and every repair is done at Mara’s workbench, keeping our little corner of the music world warm, honest, and welcoming.

Our History

The Record Room began with two friends—Elliot, a lifelong vinyl collector, and Mara, a turntable tinkerer—who shared a vision for a more personal, trustworthy place in the world of used records and repairs. After years of hunting for reliable shops and helping friends revive their record players, the idea for something better emerged during a rainy afternoon coffee break. What started as casual conversation quickly became a plan for a small, online space dedicated to honest grading, careful cleaning, and skilled repairs. Since that day, The Record Room has stayed true to its roots: a cozy, collector-friendly shop where every record and every repair gets the attention it deserves.
